About US

“Survay Land Registry System” (SLRS) is web based multi-tiered, scalable, reliable, and secure network enterprise application. The SLRS is Integrated, Geographical Information System (GIS) based decision support system to cater 24/7 services to JakLRMa agency user, state public user, special agency external user for analysis, evaluation and information of revenue records and its ancillary details like Records of Rights, Jamabandi, Girdawari, Mutation and many more....
